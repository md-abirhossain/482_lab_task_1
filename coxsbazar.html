Cox's Bazar, Bangladesh

Home to the longest natural sea beach in the world.

Why I Love It

Cox's Bazar is my favourite escape close to home. Walking along the 120 kilometres of unbroken sandy beach while the waves roll in, watching the sun melt into the Bay of Bengal, and eating fresh seafood by the shore is the kind of break everyone needs. It is peaceful, beautiful and just a short trip away.

Things To Do

🏖️ Beach Walk

Stroll along the longest natural sea beach in the world.

🌅 Watch the Sunset

Catch a glowing sunset over the Bay of Bengal from the shore.

🦐 Fresh Seafood

Enjoy grilled fish, prawns and other fresh catches by the sea.
