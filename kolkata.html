Kolkata, India

The City of Joy, full of history, culture and great food.

Why I Love It

Kolkata is a city that never lets you feel like a stranger. The old colonial buildings, the rattling yellow taxis, the smell of street food on every corner and the warmth of the people make it one of my favourite places to visit. There is art, history and a cup of cha waiting around every turn.

Things To Do

🏛️ Victoria Memorial

Walk around the grand marble monument set in beautiful gardens.

🌉 Howrah Bridge

Cross the iconic bridge over the busy Hooghly River.

🍲 Street Food

Try kathi rolls, puchka and sweet rosogolla all over the city.
